1- Immobilization of the patient when he had a seizure.
2- Do not gather around the injured person.
3- Placing any object in the mouth of the injured person or between his teeth.
4- Attempting to move the injured person during the seizure.
5- Do not panic and assume that the casualty knows what is happening or what has happened.
